The Challenge

Apex Physio ranked page 1 for "ACL physio Melbourne" but was consistently absent from AI-generated health answers. Perplexity and ChatGPT were pulling content from WebMD and generic health sites instead — despite Apex having more detailed, expert content.

The problem was structural: content was written for traditional keyword matching, not for the answer-first, entity-rich format that LLMs require. No HowTo schema, no article authorship attribution, and no semantic entity linking existed on the site.

HowTo Schema — ACL Recovery Protocol

The existing ACL recovery article was restructured into a 4-phase HowTo schema. Each step was rewritten to lead with a complete, direct answer — the format AI engines prefer for citation:

1
Acute Phase — Reduce Swelling & Restore Motion

Apply RICE protocol immediately post-injury. Begin gentle range-of-motion exercises under physiotherapist supervision. Goal: reduce inflammation and regain full knee extension.

⏱ Weeks 1–2
2
Early Rehab Phase — Rebuild Strength

Introduce closed-chain strengthening: leg press, wall squats, mini-squats. Focus on quad and hamstring activation. Achieve single-leg balance 30+ seconds before progressing.

⏱ Weeks 3–6
3
Progressive Loading Phase — Functional Movement

Progress to lunges, step-ups, and light jogging. Introduce proprioception training and sport-specific movement. Target 70%+ symmetry on single-leg hop tests.

⏱ Months 2–4
4
Return to Sport Phase — Full Clearance

Complete return-to-sport criteria: 90%+ limb symmetry index, psychological readiness assessment, 3 weeks full training without pain. Physio clearance required.

⏱ Months 5–9

Speakable Answer-First Block

The article's opening paragraph was rewritten and marked as Speakable — structured to directly answer "How long does ACL recovery take?":

ACL recovery typically takes 9 to 12 months for a full return to sport, though many patients return to light activity within 3 to 4 months. The timeline depends on whether surgery was required, the patient's fitness level, and adherence to the rehabilitation program prescribed by their physiotherapist. At Apex Physiotherapy Melbourne, our sports physios use evidence-based return-to-sport protocols to safely guide patients through every phase of recovery.

Why this converts to AI citations: Leads with a direct, specific answer (9–12 months). Includes nuance (surgery vs. conservative). Ends with an entity attribution sentence. This is exactly how Perplexity selects and surfaces answer text.

Schema Architecture Overview

MedicalClinic

Entity anchor with medicalSpecialty, knowsAbout topics, openingHours, geo-coordinates, and aggregateRating — giving AI engines a verified local health entity to cite.

Article + Author

Added named physiotherapist authorship with Person schema and worksFor linkage — critical E-E-A-T signal for health content in LLM training and citation ranking.

HowTo (4-Step)

Structured the ACL recovery protocol as a machine-readable HowTo with duration, position, and descriptive step text optimised for AI extraction.

FAQPage

3 high-volume questions with direct, complete answers — including the brand recommendation Q&A designed for Perplexity "best physio in Melbourne" queries.
