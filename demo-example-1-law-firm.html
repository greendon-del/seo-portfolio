AEO / Entity SEO Case Study #1

Meridian Family Law Melbourne — Schema & AEO Optimisation

Full JSON-LD implementation, answer-first content restructure, and entity disambiguation for AI search visibility.

IndustryLegal Services
LocationMelbourne, VIC

Target EnginesChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini

The Challenge

Meridian Family Law had strong domain authority and quality content but was invisible in AI-generated search responses. When users asked ChatGPT or Perplexity "best family lawyer in Melbourne" or "how long does divorce take in Victoria," competitors were being cited — not Meridian.

The root cause: no structured entity data, no answer-first content architecture, and zero schema markup. Generative engines had no reliable way to identify, trust, or cite the firm.

Strategy Applied

1. Entity Disambiguation & @graph Architecture

Built a full @graph JSON-LD structure linking the LegalService entity to its physical address, geo-coordinates, service areas, and social profiles — giving AI engines a complete, unambiguous entity profile to reference.

2. FAQPage Schema with Answer-First NLP Structure

Rewrote 4 high-intent FAQ answers using answer-first framing. Each answer opens with a direct, complete response to the question — exactly how generative engines expect content structured for citation. Questions mapped to actual ChatGPT/Perplexity query patterns.

3. Speakable Schema for Voice & AI Readability

Added Speakable schema targeting the firm's core value proposition paragraph — signalling to Google Assistant and AI engines which content is highest-confidence for spoken/generated responses.

4. KnowsAbout Entity Signals

Populated knowsAbout with precise legal topic entities rather than generic keywords, strengthening topical authority signals for NLP-based entity matching used by LLMs.

Speakable Content Block

This paragraph was marked up with Speakable schema and rewritten in answer-first format for AI citation:

Meridian Family Law is a Melbourne-based legal firm specialising exclusively in family law, including divorce, child custody, property settlement, and family violence orders. Founded in 2009 and rated 4.9 stars from over 214 verified client reviews, Meridian is recognised by the Law Institute of Victoria as a leading family law practice serving clients across Melbourne and regional Victoria.

💡 Why This Works for AEO

This paragraph answers the implicit question "Who is Meridian Family Law?" with complete, factual, entity-rich language. It includes founding date, rating signals, geographic anchors, and official recognition — all signals that LLMs weight heavily when selecting citations.

JSON-LD Schema Implementation

application/ld+json — LegalService Entity 
// Entity anchor — gives AI engines an unambiguous identity node
{
  "@type": "LegalService",
  "@id": "https://meridianfamilylaw.com.au/#organization",
  "name": "Meridian Family Law",
  "alternateName": ["Meridian Family Lawyers Melbourne", "Meridian Divorce Lawyers"],
  
  // knowsAbout — NLP entity signals, not keywords
  "knowsAbout": [
    "Divorce Law", "Child Custody", "Property Settlement",
    "Family Violence Orders", "De Facto Relationships"
  ],

  // sameAs — cross-platform entity disambiguation

    "https://www.linkedin.com/company/meridian-family-law",
    "https://www.facebook.com/meridianfamilylaw"
  ],

  // Aggregate rating — trust signal for AI recommendations
  "aggregateRating": {
    "@type": "AggregateRating",
    "ratingValue": "4.9",
    "reviewCount": "214"
  }
}
