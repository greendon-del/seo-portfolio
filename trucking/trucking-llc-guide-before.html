Trucking Business Info

Welcome to our website. We provide information about trucking and how to get into the trucking business. There are many things you need to know if you want to be in trucking. It can be a good business but there is a lot to do. Read below for more info and contact us if you have questions about anything related to trucks and the trucking industry today.

If you want to start in trucking you will need to do some paperwork and get set up properly. A lot of people want to know how to do this. Basically you need to register your business and get the right numbers and permits and insurance and things like that. It depends on what kind of trucking you want to do. Some people do it as a sole proprietor and some people make a company. Either way there are steps. You have to get a DOT number and also an MC number in a lot of cases and there is a fee for one of them. You also need insurance which can be expensive especially when you are new. After you apply there is a waiting period before you can start hauling loads for money.

Insurance is important and you cannot operate without it. The amount depends on what you haul. You also might need cargo insurance and other kinds. It costs money every month and it is usually more expensive in the beginning. There are also other filings like a BOC-3 and you need that before your authority is active. Some companies help you with all of this for a fee if you do not want to do it yourself.

There are also ongoing things you have to keep up with like fuel taxes and registration and taxes on heavy vehicles and drug testing programs and so on. It is a lot to keep track of. If you miss something you can get in trouble or get shut down so you have to stay on top of it. Many new companies fail because they run out of money in the first few months so you need to plan for that and have enough cash to get started and keep going until you get paid.

In conclusion, trucking can be a rewarding business but you need to do it the right way and follow all the rules and regulations. Make sure you get all your paperwork done and get insured and stay compliant. Contact us today to learn more about starting your trucking company and we can help you get on the road. Thank you for visiting our site and good luck with your trucking business.
